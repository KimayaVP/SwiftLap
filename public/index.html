ℹ️ Why these intensity levels?

Your plan's intensity is set automatically from how far your best logged time is from your active goal.

HIGHMore than 5s away from your goal — heavy race-pace & threshold work to close the gap.
MODERATEWithin 5s of your goal — speed & race-simulation sets to sharpen.
MAINTENANCEGoal already achieved — technique & consistency to hold your form.
